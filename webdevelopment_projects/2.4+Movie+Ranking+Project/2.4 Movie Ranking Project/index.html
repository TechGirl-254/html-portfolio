Welcome to Wammy's Favorite Movies Page

Here's a list of my top three favorite movies

1. Fault in Our Stars
2. Medici Masters of Florence
3. Downton Abbey

The Fault in Our Stars

For some reason, I just can't get enough of the emotions in this movie. I mean, whoever wrote the script, is a genius. The poetry in it, the choice of characters, the plot twist, I can't help but fall in love.

Medici

Medici isn't necessarily a movie, it's a series, but my oh my is it amazing. Forget the drama in Game of Thrones, Medici is where it's all at. Understaning Florentine culture, watching ancient art come to life, watching the life of a dinasty unfold, it's epic

Downton Abbet

This is also a series, set up in Elizabethan England. My goodness am I in love with the character Lady Violet Crowley. Her quick wit and eloquence is unparalled.
